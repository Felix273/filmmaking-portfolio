Work
With Us.

We're a small team that does big work. Everyone here is deeply invested in the craft — and in each other. If you're obsessed with filmmaking and want to build something meaningful in African cinema, we should talk.

4 Open Roles · Nairobi, Kenya

01 — Craft

We take the work seriously. Not ourselves.

High standards, low ego. We debate creative decisions fiercely and then move forward together. Nobody's too senior to learn and nobody's too junior to have a great idea.

02 — Autonomy

You're trusted to lead your domain.

We hire people who know what they're doing and then let them do it. No micromanagement. No permission structures for every small decision. Ownership is real here.

03 — Rootedness

African perspective is our competitive advantage.

We're not a local outpost of a global agency. We're a Nairobi-born company telling African stories to the world. That context shapes how we work and what we make.

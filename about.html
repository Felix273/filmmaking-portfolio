Our Story

Born from a love of the moving image.

Founded in Nairobi in 2020, FOB Media was born from a simple conviction: that Africa has stories worth telling, and the world is ready to hear them.

We started as a small team of three — a director, a cinematographer, and a producer — working out of a single office in Westlands. What we lacked in resources, we made up for in obsession. Every frame mattered. Every cut had to earn its place.

Five years later, we've grown into a full-service production house with a team of 24 creatives, technicians, and strategists. We've shot in 12 countries, worked with global brands and independent artists alike, and built a body of work we're genuinely proud of.

But the conviction hasn't changed. We still believe the best work comes from taking the time to understand what a story is really about — and then finding the most cinematic way to tell it.

How We
Work

Four principles guide every project we take on — from a 30-second commercial to a feature-length documentary. They're not rules. They're the way we think.

01

Story First,
Always

Before we talk about cameras or locations or budgets, we ask: what is this story really about? The answer shapes every decision that follows.

02

Craft Over
Convenience

The easy choice is rarely the right choice. We push past the first idea, the obvious shot, the safe edit — because our clients deserve the best version, not the fastest one.

03

Rooted in
Africa

We bring an African perspective to every project — not as a niche, but as a strength. Our context, our eye, our network is an asset to every client we work with.

04

Honest
Partnership

We tell clients what we think, not what they want to hear. The best work comes from honest creative collaboration, and that only happens when trust goes both ways.
